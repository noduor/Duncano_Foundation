About Duncano Foundation

Learn about our journey, mission, and the team dedicated to transforming African communities through education

Our History

Founded in 2017, the Duncano Foundation is the Philanthropic Social Investment arm of Duncan Oduor Otieno and his Family as well as associated businesses.

We are funded by family and associated businesses; various donor partners; other philanthropic organizations and individuals around the world, that share the Foundation's beliefs and philosophy.

We design our initiatives around all key areas of education empowerment and sustainable support across Africa.

At the Duncano Foundation, we are committed to unlocking the potential of African communities through strategic social investments in education.

Our Mission

To promote innovative leadership and entrepreneurship for sustainable development by expanding access to quality education, breaking down barriers of inequality, and opening doors to life-changing opportunities.

Our Vision

To see all-inclusive communities where individuals are educated, empowered, and equipped to drive sustainable development across Africa.

How We Operate

We operate through strategic partnerships, focusing on comprehensive programs that provide both immediate support and long-term sustainable development.

Our Team

Meet the dedicated individuals leading our mission to transform education in Africa

Duncan Oduor

Co-Founder & Chairman

Brings visionary leadership and a deep passion for community transformation. Under his leadership, the foundation has spearheaded numerous initiatives aimed at improving education for underprivileged communities in Africa.

Caroline Oduor

Co-Founder & Vice Chairperson

Plays a pivotal role in shaping the foundation's strategic initiatives. Her commitment to gender equality and women's empowerment influences the foundation's programs, ensuring they are inclusive and address the needs of the most vulnerable.

Dr. Richard Ronoh

Country Director General - Kenya

Responsible for overseeing all of the foundation's operations within Kenya. His expertise in strategic management and program development ensures the effective implementation of the foundation's projects and initiatives.
